Diet Plan Overview

This comprehensive diet plan is designed for a 65 kg individual aiming to become a fit young athlete. The plan provides 2,200–2,400 kcal daily through 6 balanced meals, with an emphasis on high protein, moderate carbs, and healthy fats to support muscle growth and recovery.

Supplements & Hydration

Daily Supplements

  • • Whey Protein: 2 servings daily
  • • Creatine Monohydrate: 5g daily
  • • Multivitamin: 1 serving
  • • Omega-3: 1000mg daily

Hydration Guidelines

  • • Minimum 2.5-3 liters of water daily
  • • Additional 500ml during workouts
  • • Monitor urine color (pale yellow ideal)
  • • Limit caffeine intake after 4 PM

Nutrition Tips

Meal Prep Tips

  • • Prepare meals in advance for 2-3 days
  • • Use food scale for accurate portions
  • • Store meals in airtight containers
  • • Keep healthy snacks readily available

General Guidelines

  • • Eat every 2.5-3 hours
  • • Chew food thoroughly
  • • Avoid processed foods
  • • Adjust portions based on progress
